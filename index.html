WHO I AM

I'm focused on Physical AI—moving beyond LLMs to address real-world challenges in road safety and infrastructure. While others chase virtual assistants, I'm building systems that interact with the physical world, from high-frequency accelerometer data classification to real-time telemetry visualization. I'm a Class of 2027 MIT applicant with 90%+ in CBSE boards, but my real education happens at the intersection of computer vision and urban mobility.

My mission is to bridge the gap between digital intelligence and physical reality on the roads of Ghaziabad and beyond. I don't just build models—I build solutions that save lives and improve infrastructure, one pothole at a time.
