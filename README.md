India has 200M+ motorcycles and one of the world's highest road fatality rates. Most riders have no access to smart safety systems that car owners take for granted. I want to change that — starting with software, eventually with hardware.

**Long term:** a startup building affordable AI safety systems for two-wheelers in emerging markets.
